IGI2 Covert Strike

IGI 2: Covert Strike is a tactical FPS sequel where players control David Jones, an ex-SAS soldier. Assigned to prevent international crises, the game features stealth missions in global locations. Players use tactics to rescue hostages, eliminate targets, and steal valuable information. Enhanced graphics and AI provide a immersive experience. Multiplayer mode allows for competitive matches. Released in 2003, it continues the engaging IGI storyline.
