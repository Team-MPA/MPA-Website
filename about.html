About MPA

MPA, short for MultiPlayer Alive, isn't just a gaming community—it's a resurrection. At present, they've defied the odds, bringing the pulse-pounding action of IGI 2's multiplayer back to life. MPA isn't satisfied with mere nostalgia; they've forged a hub where battle-hardened veterans and wide-eyed newcomers converge. This is more than a revival; it's a movement. MPA showcases the enduring allure of multiplayer mayhem, proving that when passion meets innovation, even the most cherished gaming experiences can be eternal. Join MPA now and rediscover the adrenaline, the tactics, and the camaraderie that only multiplayer gaming can offer.
